Terms of Service

  • Please check the F.A.Q before asking me a question
  • I have the right to post personnal art between commissions.
  • Full payment is required before I begin your commission. Payments are accepted via PayPal only, in EUR. The client is responsible for covering any currency conversion fees.
  • My art style is naturally sketchy, so please don't expect a fully clean or polished finish unless you choose add the +40% option for better render/ less sketchy.
  • Product will be delivered digitally.
  • I reserve the right to refuse any commission request for any reason.
  • Work on your commission will begin after I send you the initial sketch and receive confirmation.
  • I do not accept commissions with deadlines. Completion usually takes 1-2 weeks after the sketch is started.
  • During holidays, progress may be slower, so please be patient.
  • Full payment is required before I begin working on your commission, depending on my current queue.
  • Once your commission is finished, I will ask for your email or Toyhouse account to send the high-quality version.
  • The customer does not have the right to use the commissioned artwork in any AI-related technology, including but not limited to training datasets, machine learning models, or any future AI-based technologies.

If you have any questions, feel free to ask! :D

F.A.Q

Do you make reference sheets?

Yes, I do offer reference sheets. Since they're not something I do regularly, please provide a plan or example image showing what you'd like included. We can then discuss pricing based on that.

When will you open commissions again?

I reopen commissions once my current queue is cleared, usually every 2–3 months.

How can I contact you?

You can reach me via Instagram DMs or by email at venuxiio@outlook.fr

When can I expect my commission to be completed?

As I work on multiple commissions at once, you can track your commission's progress through the commission status tab.
